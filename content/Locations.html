Meet us at one of two convenient locations

Hays & Skerry, Boston

22 Batterymarch Street, Boston, MA 02109

Telephone: 617-423-5599


ARRIVING BY FOOT

Hays & Skerry Boston is easy walking distance from anywhere in the Financial District, at the intersection of Milk and Batterymarch Streets. Please enter via the front door of the building and proceed up the stairs to the mezzanine.


ARRIVING BY PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION

Red line: Exit the Red Line at Downtown Crossing. Head up Washington Street until you reach Water Street. Once at Liberty Square, Batterymarch Street will be to your right and 22 Batterymarch will be on your left.

Orange and Blue lines: Exit at the State Street stop. Proceed through Devonshire PL and take a right onto Devonshire Street. Take a left onto Water Street and go three blocks, into Liberty Square, Batterymarch street is on your right and the building will be on your left.

Green line: Exit at Government Center and proceed east on Court Street. Proceed 5 blocks until you reach Kilby Street. Follow Kilby Street for two blocks until you reach Liberty Square. Bear left onto Batterymarch Street. 22 Batterymarch is on your left.


ARRIVING BY CAR

From the south:

  • Follow MA-3 N and I-93 N to exit 23 (North St) in Boston.
  • Take exit 23 from I-93 N and continue on North St. Take Congress St. to Batterymarch St.

From the west:

  • Follow I-90 E to Boston. Take the exit on the left toward South Station.
  • Merge onto Atlantic Ave.
  • Turn left onto Pearl St.
  • Turn right onto Water St.
  • Take the 2nd right onto Batterymarch St.

From the north:

  • Continue on US-1 S to Boston. Take exit 23 I-93 S
  • Take Oliver St. to Batterymarch St.
