Work with Trusted Experts in Business Commercial Law

Hays & Skerry provides quality legal representation to privately owned businesses, family businesses, nonprofits and other corporate and business entities. The firm has extensive experience representing clients in complex transactional matters and provides comprehensive legal services across a range of business needs. In addition, the firm provides counsel on the formation of business entities; structuring and implementing business relationships, including tax issues; real estate and commercial loan transactions; and general business matters.

With 75 years of combined experience, a wealth of legal knowledge and a well-established reputation, attorneys William C Hays and Jeffrey J.
