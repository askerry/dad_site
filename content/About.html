About the Firm


Hays & Skerry is proud to carry forward a long and distinguished tradition of providing outstanding legal counsel to individuals, business, institutions and non-profit clients. Before welcoming Jeff Skerry into the firm, William Hays practiced with distinction for many years as successor to the practice of the Honorable Bert E. Holland (1866-1958), Whitfield Johnson (1897-1995), and the Honorable William E. Hays (1903-1999).

The philosophy of our practice is a simple one. We want to understand the law first, our clients' personal needs next, and then integrate the two into intelligent and practical legal solutions. The advice and counsel we offer is built on an understanding not only of the law, but of the many highly idiosyncratic factors that influence the direction of legal advice and counsel.

At Hays and Skerry, we offer clients both the sophisticated solutions of a large firm together with the commitment to personal service often reserved for smaller firms.
