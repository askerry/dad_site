Business & Commercial Law

Work with trusted experts in business & commercial Law

Hays & Skerry provides quality legal representation to privately owned and family businesses, to nonprofits, and to other corporate and business entities. We have extensive experience representing clients in complex transactional matters and provide comprehensive legal services to meet the needs of a modern business. At Hays & Skerry, our goal is to eliminate the barriers that separate your business from its objectives, and replace them with effective legal solutions. We are dedicated to offering our clients advice and counsel for a range of transational business law matters, including:

Comprehensive business legal services

  • Choice of entity analysis
  • Business formation and maintenance
  • Equity structures
  • Bank financing
  • Business lending and financing
  • Contract review and negotiation
  • Joint ventures
  • Partnership taxation
  • Employment agreements
  • Buy/sell agreements
  • Succession planning
  • Business acquisition and sales
  • Mergers and transfers
  • Tax planning
  • Lease reviews

We appreciate the level of financial commitment and personal dedication that business owners put into these types of transactions. We want our clients to be as involved as they wish to be in all phases of the process. Our attorneys will take the time to fully explain the process and identify your options so you can make well-informed decisions with regard to the legal needs of your business.
