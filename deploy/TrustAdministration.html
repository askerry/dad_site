Trust Administration

Trusted counsel for trustees and beneficieries

With an effective trust arrangement, you can not only secure and control around the management and distribution of your assets, but also minimize losses to taxation, protect financial privacy, and save beneficiaries the costs and hassles of probate. At Hays & Skerry, we provide comprehensive, expert legal counsel to both Trustees and Trust beneficiaries. Hays and Skerry attorneys have extensive experience drafting and managing complex trusts, and put great value on handling clients' affairs with care and discretion. Where appropriate, attorneys at Hays and Skerry will also serve as Trustee on trusts of our valued clients.

  • Drafting Trust Instruments
  • Lifetime Revocable Trusts
  • Grantor-Retained Interest Trusts (GRATs and GRUTs)
  • Life Insurance Trusts
  • Trusts for the home or other real property
  • Management of Trust Portfolios
  • Fiduciary Income Tax Return Preparation
  • Prepare and Distribute Periodic Reports to Trustees and Beneficiaries
  • Making Periodic or Discretionary Distributions to Beneficiaries
  • Advising Trustees Concerning Duties Under Trust Instrument and Under Trust Law
  • Assisting with Trustee succession
  • Assisting with Tax-exempt Trusts
