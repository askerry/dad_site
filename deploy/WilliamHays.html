William C. Hays

William Hays has extensive experience in all aspects of estate planning, in the administration of trusts and in the settlement of estates. His practice also extends into the fields of real estate and general business law. Bill acts as trustee on many family trusts and also serves on the boards of numerous charitable and civic organizations.

Bill has lectured extensively in eastern Massachusetts on estate planning and affordable housing. He is past chairman of the Regional Planning Subcommittee and the Land-Use Committee of the Boston Bar Association. He is a graduate of Bowdoin College and earned his law degree from Harvard Law School. The Martindale Hubbell National Law Directory has awarded Mr. Hays an AV Rating; the highest rating for legal ability and professional ethics.
